Frequently Asked Questions

What types of clients do you work with?

We work with a diverse range of clients, from beginners looking to establish a fitness routine to experienced athletes aiming for performance enhancement. Our programmes are adaptable to various fitness levels and goals.

How long are the training sessions?

Standard training sessions are typically 60 minutes. This allows for a comprehensive warm-up, main workout, and cool-down, ensuring effectiveness and safety.

Do you offer nutritional guidance?

Yes, nutritional guidance is an integral part of our coaching. We provide personalised advice and strategies to complement your training and support your overall health goals.

What if I have an injury?

We can often work around minor injuries with modified exercises. For more significant issues, we recommend consulting with a medical professional first. We can then collaborate with your physiotherapist or doctor to ensure a safe return to training.

How quickly will I see results?

Results vary based on individual consistency, effort, and starting point. However, most clients begin to notice significant improvements in strength, energy, and body composition within 4-6 weeks of consistent training and adherence to their programme.
